The New Era of ⚡METAL⚡

The band Zed Leppelin, unsurprisingly took its name from the famous band Led Zeppelin - in what started out as a piss take - but ended up as a very successful band in its own right. The band crashed its way onto the metal scene in 2008 with their debut album 'Sell Satan'.

The band experienced a lull in their career when they changed band members and struggled to find their place in the scene. But post-covid, people came back to music in a BIG WAY and Zed Leppelin sky-rocketed in the charts with their latest album 'Mouse Trap'.

Original band members Joey and Blaxall met in the early 2000's at a house party and knew they wanted to shred together - the band welcomed two more members AJ and Rio in 2020 after they saw them play in a part-time grunge band.
